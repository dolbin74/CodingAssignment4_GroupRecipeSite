Ingredients

  • 1 pound Boneless Chuck Roast
  • Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/2 cup beef broth
  • 1 packet seasoning mix
  • 1 stick unsalted butter
  • 8 whole pepperoncini

Directions

  1. Season roast with salt, pepper, and flour.
  2. Sear in hot oil until browned.
  3. Place in slow cooker with broth, seasoning, butter, and pepperoncini.
  4. Cook on low 7–8 hours, shred, and serve.
